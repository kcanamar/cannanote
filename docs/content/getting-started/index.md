# Getting Started with CannaNote

Welcome to CannaNote! This guide will help you get set up and start tracking your cannabis consumption patterns in under 5 minutes.

## What is CannaNote?

CannaNote is a privacy-first cannabis consumption tracker that helps you:

- **Log sessions quickly** - Record your consumption in under 30 seconds
- **Discover patterns** - Learn which strains and methods work best for you
- **Stay in control** - Track your usage without judgment
- **Keep your data private** - Your information stays on your device

## Quick Setup

### Step 1: Join the Beta

1. Visit the [CannaNote homepage](/)
2. Scroll to the beta signup section
3. Enter your email address
4. Agree to our privacy-focused terms
5. Click "Request Beta Access"

### Step 2: Wait for Access

We're currently in private beta. You'll receive an email when your access is ready. Beta invites are sent weekly.

### Step 3: Download the App

Once you receive your invitation:

1. **Mobile users**: Download from the App Store or Google Play
2. **Web users**: Access directly at [app.cannanote.com](https://app.cannanote.com)

### Step 4: Create Your Account

1. Open CannaNote
2. Sign up with your invited email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Complete the privacy setup wizard

## Privacy First Approach

### Local Storage

Your consumption data is stored locally on your device first. This means:

- ✅ Your data works offline
- ✅ Faster app performance
- ✅ You control your information
- ✅ No server dependency for core features

### Optional Cloud Sync

Cloud synchronization is **completely optional** and requires explicit consent:

- Sync across your devices
- Encrypted with your password
- You can disable anytime
- Full data export available
